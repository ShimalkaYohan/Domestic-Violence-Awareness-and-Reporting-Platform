Our Services

Legal Services

Strong Heart's victims are provided free legal advice and are represented in court as and when required. These victims are made aware of their legal rights and legal position.

Psychological Counseling

All the victims are primarily directed to a counsellor, giving them the opportunity to share their stories, and making them feel safe and trusted.

Emergency Shelters

Strong Heart shelters, located in Colombo, Matara and Batticaloa, are safe havens for abused victims - who are in need of special care and psychological support.

24 Hour Caller Hotline

Strong Heart's hotline accommodates calls from Sinhala, Tamil and English.

Mobile App

Strong Heart has introduced the first ever mobile app addressing gender based violence in Sri Lanka named Break the Silence.
