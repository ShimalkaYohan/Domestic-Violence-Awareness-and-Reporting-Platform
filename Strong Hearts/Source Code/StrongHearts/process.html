Work Process

Step 01

Getting your complaint

The first step is to get your bad or dark experiences to us in any medium, for which you can send the information through mail, letter or our submission form.

Step 02

Contacting you back

As a second step, we will analyze the information you have sent us and contact you again as soon as possible. Because time is the most valuable factor in such a situation, we will work to provide our service to you as efficiently as possible.

Step 03

Referring you to support groupes

As a third step, after we bring you to our office or contact you by phone, we will provide maximum support to the support groups related to your question or our lawyers for related legal matters and continue the work ahead.
